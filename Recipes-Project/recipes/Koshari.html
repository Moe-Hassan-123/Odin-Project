Koshari

Description

Koshari is THE Egyptian dish, It's the most famous national dish in Egypt.

It's composition is 70% carbohydrate 20% fat 10% protein

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Macaroni
  • Legumes

Steps

  1. Cook the Rice
  2. Cook the macaroni
  3. Cook Legumes
  4. Add All of them together!
