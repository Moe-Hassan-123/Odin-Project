Scrambled Eggs

Description

Scrambled Eggs are universal, Every culture has it.

It's composition is mostly protein so its very healthy!!

Ingredients

  • Necessary Ingredients
    • Eggs
    • Love
  • Optional Ingredients
    • Onions
    • Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Break the eggs in a dish
  2. Scramble the eggs in the dish
  3. (Optional) Add the Onions and Tomatoes to the pan for 3 mins
  4. Pour the eggs in the pan
  5. Keep stirring the eggs
  6. Once it's cooked put the eggs in a dish
  7. Enjoy!
